About The Site

This website was established by Shay Friedman, a 19-year-old young man, for a college project.
The primary purpose of this site is to serve as a platform to manage and control the Internet of Things (IoT) components associated with the project.
On this site, users have the capability to configure and adjust all the settings related to the ESP components with ease.
we offer professional security camera services. Our cameras are equipped with advanced facial recognition technology that allows them to detect
and identify individuals who come into view. When a person stands in front of the camera, the system scans their face and compares it to a predefined
database of recognized faces. If the camera recognizes the person, it will trigger the front door to automatically open, providing a convenient and secure access experience.



In addition our company offers the provision of sterile field services, which is defined as a designated area that is equipped with an advanced sensor
system that can detect the presence of an individual. In the event that a person is detected within this area, a warning will immediately be
issued to the person to alert them of the potential risks and dangers associated with being in a sterile field.
This ensures the maintenance of a highly controlled and sanitary environment that is free from contamination and is essential in various
industries such as healthcare, scientific research, and more.
